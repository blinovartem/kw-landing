KeyWords

Browser extension for quickly copying keywords from Shutterstock

Install for Chrome
Works in Opera, Vivaldi, Edge, Brave and other Chromium browsers
For authors

Created specifically for contributors who need to quickly transfer keywords to other stocks.

Completely free

The extension is free. You can support the developer with a donation if it helps with their work.

Updated regularly

Multi-language support, continuous improvements, and adaptation to changes.

How does this work
1.Open any image, vector, illustration, or video on Shutterstock
2. Click on the extension icon
3. Click the "Get keywords" button
4. Click "Copy" - done!
It's very simple. Open the page → click on the extension → get and copy the keywords.

Clicking the play button will load the YouTube player.
